Class of 2020 Service Project

Parking Lot B (follow your Cougar Guide)
9:15 a.m.-1 p.m.
Can one person make a difference…make the world a better place? How about 2,000? The Class of 2020 will put the SIUE value of Citizenship into action today, joining upper-class students, faculty, and staff to complete hands-on projects that will make a difference to communities in the Metro East. You will get a FREE T-SHIRT…but please wear appropriate clothing:

  • closed toe shoes (athletic shoes or work boots)
  • durable clothing (preferably no shorts)
  • hat and/or sun protection, or rain gear
  • sunscreen and insect repellant
  • for your safety, NO flip flops, opentoed shoes or sandals
Contact your Cougar Guide if you need an accommodation to participate.

What should I bring to Springboard to Success?

You will need to bring your student ID number (found in your admission packet), photo identification (such as a driver's license or state-issued identification card), any personal items required for an overnight stay (we provide linens and towels, but other than this, if you need it to start or end your day, please bring it with you), comfortable clothing and shoes (you will be walking around campus), and a sweatshirt or jacket.
