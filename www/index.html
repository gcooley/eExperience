Schedule

Thursday, August 18, 2016

Bluff/Prairie/Woodland Halls

The SIUE Experience begins for many new freshmen today! Hundreds of Move-In Crew volunteers make this process incredibly smooth so you’ll be on your way to unpack and unwind in no time. More information about University Housing Move-In is available at siue.edu/housing/movein. Share your move-in pics using #SIUE20 and #SIUEExperience!

Bluff/Prairie/Woodland Halls

Check with your Resident Assistant (RA) for location Living on campus is about more than just a place to sleep and study. This required orientation will help you be successful living in a new community. Check with your RA for speci c time and location.

Morris University Center (MUC)

Living off campus this year? It is important to us that you are fully able to participate in the SIUE Experience! Come meet other new commuter students and then head downstairs to enjoy Freshman First Night together.

Morris University Center (MUC)

Class of 2020, join fellow rst-year students for an unforgettable night packed with laughter, glowing entertainment and the best food on campus! Experience the MUC’s many dining options (bring your Cougar Card with meal plan or cash). Step into the Illumabooth to create a glow photo or dress up with props at the open-air photo booth. Get a sugar rush from cotton candy or escape into the video game lounge. Be sure to stop by Cougar Lanes for a game of pool or Cosmic Bowling. Feeling crafty? Decorate a picture frame to remember the event, or make your own Spin Art creation. At the end of the night, show off your dance moves during the DJ dance party! Post pics using #FFN2016 #SIUE20 #SIUEExperience

Friday, August 19, 2016

Morris University Center (MUC)

Enjoy campus dining options like Center Court and Starbucks to fuel up for a busy day of The SIUE Experience. Meal plans, cash and credit cards accepted.

MUC Fixins Restaurant

If you are living off campus this year, make this your rst stop for a light breakfast and information about The SIUE Experience! Meet your Cougar Guide, along with other commuter students, and enter a drawing for gas gift cards and other cool prizes.
